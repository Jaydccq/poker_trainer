# Notes: Mobile-First Arrangement Flow

## Sources

### UI/UX Pro Max Searches
- Product: Mobile card game
  - Style direction: retro-futurism, vibrant gaming palette
- UX: Mobile first guidance
  - Prefer single-focus flow and thumb-friendly bottom tray

## Synthesized Findings
- On mobile, show one zone at a time via horizontal swipe to reduce cognitive load.
- Keep the unassigned hand tray fixed at the bottom with horizontal scrolling.
